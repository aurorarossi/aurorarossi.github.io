Hello!

I'm Aurora Rossi.

I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Bonn and Lamarr Institute in the group of Prof. Dr. Petra Mutzel.

I completed my PhD at DS4H Université Côte d’Azur in the COATI project joint-team between Inria centre at Université Côte d’Azur and the I3S Laboratory under the supervision of David Coudert, DR.

My research focuses on graph theory and graph-based machine learning, with applications in chemistry and neuroscience.
